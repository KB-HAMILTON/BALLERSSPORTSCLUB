Aims

Ballers sports Club is dedicated to training and develop Volleyballs at every level. We provide tactical development of fundamental skills and other essential skills. Our training is crafted to accomodate everyone. Our method of coaching is sciences based and devekoped by an Internal F.I.V.B coach.

We aim to:

  1. To maintain the number 1 ranking in the country as a club.
  2. To create holistic,coachable and disciplined players.
  3. To maintain growth within the club.
